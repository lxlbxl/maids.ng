Maids.ng is Nigeria's leading AI-powered domestic staff matching platform. We connect employers with verified housekeepers, nannies, cooks, drivers, elderly carers, cleaners, laundry staff, and home managers across all major Nigerian cities.

## What we do
- NIN-verified identity check for every domestic worker
- AI-powered matching based on employer needs, schedule, location, and budget
- 10-day money-back guarantee on all matching fee payments
- Full salary guides and hiring advice for every city in Nigeria

## Cities we serve
Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, Kano, Enugu, Benin City, Warri, Owerri, Uyo, Calabar — and surrounding towns.

## Services offered
Housekeepers, Nannies, Cooks, Drivers, Elderly Carers, Cleaners, Laundry Persons, Home Managers.
